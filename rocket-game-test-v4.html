How to Play

  • Click anywhere in the text area to activate rocket mode
  • Arrow Keys to control the rocket:
    • ‚Üë - Accelerate forward
    • ‚Üì - Brake/Reverse
    • ‚Üê - Turn left
    • ‚Üí - Turn right
  • Escape to exit rocket mode
  • Avoid hitting text characters - only spaces, tabs, and newlines are safe
  • Points increase while flying, reset to 0 when rocket disappears
