Rocket Text Game v2 - Health & Points System

How to Play:

  • Click in any textarea below to activate rocket mode
  • Arrow Keys: Up (accelerate), Down (brake), Left/Right (turn)
  • Safe Flight: Fly through spaces, tabs, and newlines to recover health
  • Avoid Letters: Hitting letters causes damage and blinking effect
  • Points: Earn points for safe flight time while moving
  • Escape: Press ESC to exit rocket mode

Health System

100% health that decreases on letter collision and recovers when flying safely

Blinking Effect

Rocket blinks when health drops below 30% as a warning

Points System

Earn points for safe flight time, displayed above the rocket

Safe Spaces

Spaces, tabs, and newlines are safe to fly through
